🤓 Create Your Own Font

Since generating a font directly from a photo is highly complex, you can easily use a free external tool to do the magic:

  1. Visit Calligraphr.com and create a free account.
  2. Download and print their PDF template grid.
  3. Write your letters in the printed boxes, and take a clear photo of the page.
  4. Upload that photo to Calligraphr. They will process it and provide you with a .ttf file of your handwriting!
  5. Return to this page and upload that .ttf file using the Upload your handwriting font button.
